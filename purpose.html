Purpose & Strategic Priorities

This document sets out the strategies and support used across Nova Education Trust primary schools to create transformational schools.

Our Purpose

Creating transformational schools

This Playbook shows where we are consistent, so that alignment across our primary schools adds genuine value.

It ensures all school leaders, staff and our central team understand how we are making our vision of transformational schools a reality in our early years and primary settings. Many elements are consistent with practice in our secondary schools and sixth form provision, but the focus of this document is alignment across our primary schools.

Alignment across our primary schools adds genuine value. Each school exercises professional autonomy to choose the tactics that fit local need — but nobody is solving problems alone.

One vision, shared across all primaries

Every Nova primary works to the same broad SIF aims and goals — aligned in strategy, autonomous in tactics, never isolated in improvement.

Nova Strategy 2026–2030

Three pillars at the heart of our strategy

The Nova Strategy 2026–2030 organises the Trust’s work around three pillars: Teamwork, Innovation and Results. Each pillar has a guiding tagline, mission statement and three KPIs, and sits above a set of annual priorities that describe the specific work for that year.

1

Teamwork — “Belong. Attend. Contribute.”

Building the culture, relationships and systems that let people and schools thrive. KPIs: pupil and staff belonging scores in the top national quartile; pupil attendance exceeding the national average; at least 90% of staff recommending Nova as a workplace.

2

Innovation — “Grow. Improve. Simplify.”

Developing expertise, sharpening practice and finding better ways of working. KPIs: 100% of staff completing development pathways with at least 90% reporting that CPD improves their practice; a named successor identified for every senior role; measurable annual reduction in administrative workload.

3

Results — “Achieve. Develop. Flourish.”

Delivering outcomes and preparing children for their futures. KPIs: attainment and progress above the national average across all phases; 100% of pupils accessing the full enrichment entitlement; positive destinations above the national average for all groups.

Supporting all three pillars are six enablers: People, Governance, Finance, Infrastructure, Compliance and Risk. These are the non-strategic foundations that keep the Trust running effectively. The Primary Playbook is the operational expression of the strategy in primary settings.
