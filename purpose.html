Purpose & Strategic Priorities

This document sets out the strategies and support used across Nova Education Trust primary schools to create transformational schools.

Our Purpose

Creating transformational schools

This Playbook shows where we are consistent, so that alignment across our primary schools adds genuine value.

It ensures all school leaders, staff and our central team understand how we are making our vision of transformational schools a reality in our early years and primary settings. Many elements are consistent with practice in our secondary schools and sixth form provision, but the focus of this document is alignment across our primary schools.

Alignment across our primary schools adds genuine value. Each school exercises professional autonomy to choose the tactics that fit local need — but nobody is solving problems alone.

One vision, shared across all primaries

Every Nova primary works to the same broad SIF aims and goals — aligned in strategy, autonomous in tactics, never isolated in improvement.

Strategic Priorities

Three priorities at the heart of the Nova mission

These enable us to ensure all schools in Nova Education Trust are aligned on their goals. In primary, these broad goals drive our school improvement framework.

1

Ambition and Inclusion

Removing barriers, unleashing potential and improving lives.

2

Advocacy and Innovation

Nurturing creativity, discovery and experiment.

3

Culture

Culture and solutions.

The Five Year Strategic Plan sets out these goals in more detail. The Playbook is the operational expression of those goals in primary settings.
