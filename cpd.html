Section 8

CPD & Networks

Trust alignment through shared CPD, subject networks, external partnerships and a structured accreditation pathway for every member of staff.

Shared CPD

Common goals, aligned strategies, expert support

As the primary schools in Nova share many of the same goals, we have worked to create aligned strategies and accessed transformational trainers, leaders and organisations beyond Nova.

Guerrilla Curriculum Group

Since 2025 Nova has partnered with Jonathon Lear (Guerrilla Education) to deliver our USP through curriculum design. Group sessions and 1:1 support enable all schools to have transformational curriculums. Work directly informs Curriculum QA visits.

Botherdness

From summer 2026, a partnership with Hywel Roberts implements Botherdness into the ILP and curriculum. Each school’s Botherdness Lead attends a 5-day programme. Schools access the CPD platform, Nova-specific webinars and AI planning tools.

Reading, Writing & Maths Networks

Leaders meet separately to bring the Nova Playbooks to life. Each subject has a Strategic Leadership facilitator. Meetings take place online after school twice per half-term for the first 6 months, then frequency reduces as embedding continues.

East Midlands East Maths Hub

All schools access support and CPD through the Maths Hub. Schools have selected complementary CPD courses to ensure alignment. Maths Leaders within schools are progressing to become Primary Maths Specialists.

Flying High English Hub

Nova works with the English Hub on Early Reading and Phonics. Each school completes an annual audit which informs action planning. The Hub provides additional training and is broadening support beyond Early Reading and Phonics.

Cross-Phase Networks

Nova Primaries staff attend cross-phase networks for SEND, Safeguarding, Attendance and Personal Development. Each School/Business Manager attends the Trust Business and Operations Manager Network.

ILP Accreditation Pathway

From webinar to Nova Advanced Skills Teacher

We have designed a series of webinars supporting staff in understanding the ILP Framework, with formal accreditation at every stage.

1

Accessibility & Adaptation Webinars

All staff complete these webinars and reflection activities, resulting in formal accreditation of engagement with this CPD.

2

ILP Strand Webinars

A series of webinars focused on the four ILP strands: Direct Instruction, Independent Practice, Assessment and Feedback.

3

Strand Accreditation

After completing webinars, tasks and reflections, staff can request assessment. Successful candidates are accredited in the relevant ILP strand.

4

Nova Advanced Skills Teacher

Once accredited in all strands, staff work towards Nova AST status. This is a rigorous process carrying additional remuneration and responsibility for modelling teaching and coaching others. Reassessed every 2 years.

Teaching Assistants can also work towards ILP accreditation where appropriate. A series of webinars and CPD led by support staff will be delivered in 2026–27 showcasing specialist knowledge and expertise. Nova will also work with Matt Parker (Maximising the Impact of Teaching Assistants) to complement this work.

Headteacher Development

Growing leaders who grow others

School visits

Every headteacher visits at least 2 other Nova Primary schools each year. Each year all headteachers visit an exemplary setting outside of Nova as a group.

Half-termly online meetings

Online meetings for Primary headteachers every half-term to share key updates and explore emerging themes across the Trust.

Face-to-face training

Two Face-to-Face Primary Headteacher Training mornings and two Joint Primary and Secondary Headteacher Training Days per year.

QA participation

Every headteacher supports at least one QA in another school over the year. There is also opportunity to support QA in other trusts as part of growth and development.
