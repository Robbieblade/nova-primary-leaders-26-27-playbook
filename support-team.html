Section 4

Primary Support Team

Alongside the Director of School Improvement for Primary, a dedicated team provides intensive, regular support across all Nova primaries.

Central Operations

The platform for transformational education

The central operations team — led by Michael Davies (Chief Finance and Operations Officer) and supported by the Directors of IT, Operations and People — supports schools with all non-education related matters.

Staff from Finance, People, IT and Data Services, Health, Safety and Compliance, Governance and Premises routinely work with schools to ensure they have the platform to deliver a transformational experience safely and sustainably. The Trust Safeguarding Lead and Trust Attendance Lead provide cross-phase support.

Primary-Specific Roles

Intensive, named support for every primary

Primary Inclusion Lead

Rachel Mousley — 3.5 days per week

Rachel provides strategic leadership and oversight of SEND provision across schools, ensuring that all pupils with SEND have equitable access to high-quality education. Support is bespoke and responsive while maintaining a consistent and robust approach across the wider provision.

Each primary school receives:

  • Minimum of two planned visits per half term including initial strategic planning meeting with SLT
  • Quality Assurance with written feedback and action planning
  • Statutory SEND compliance checks (including EHCP processes and SEND registers)
  • Support and preparation for Ofsted inspections, and onsite during inspections
  • Annual Medical Needs and First Aid audit
  • Induction and mentoring for SENCOs new to role
  • Termly SEND newsletters with updates, guidance and best practice
  • SENCo networks and collaboration opportunities
  • Access to a shared SEND Handbook and SEND Resource Bank
  • Coaching to support effective provision mapping and impact evaluation
  • Guidance on parental engagement and multi-agency working

ILP, Curriculum and Achievement Lead

Dave Edwards — 2.5 days per week

Dave drives implementation of aligned approaches to teaching and learning across Nova Primaries, bringing the Primary Strategy to life through the ILP framework, Playbooks and common achievement tracking systems.

  • Works with external partners: Sync, Magma Maths, Showbie, Olex and Microsoft
  • Works with the Lead Innovator and Innovators to ensure consistency in digital tool use
  • Analyses data sources and undertakes QA to identify barriers
  • Develops curriculum playbooks in foundation subjects; contributes to core subject revisions
  • Provides CPD for staff across the primaries
  • Collates and produces the Monthly Nova Primaries Magazine ‘Supercharged’
  • Leads development of Quest Progress, Olex AI, Reading Progress, Magma Maths, Showbie Assessments
  • Supports AI and workflow development across Nova Trust

ILP Lead Innovator

Charlotte Pindar: half a day a week across Nova primary schools

Charlotte leads the development and implementation of Apple learning tools across Nova primaries. Her role focuses on the onboarding of new schools into the ILP framework and on how Kaligo and Showbie are used to support learning effectively.

  • Development of Apple learning tools across Nova primary schools
  • Onboarding of new schools into the Nova ILP framework
  • Supporting schools in how Kaligo and Showbie are used to support learning

ILP Innovators

One per school

A member of staff in each school is identified as the school’s Innovator, responsible for driving the use of digital technology tools to amplify teaching and learning through the ILP framework. They meet half-termly with Innovators from other Nova primaries and work with national partners. They receive access to CPD and tools in advance of other staff.

Early Years Lead

Kelly Ryan — 1 day per week

Kelly supports headteachers, Early Years leaders and staff across the Trust to ensure awareness of the latest EYFS guidance and best practice.

  • Providing and signposting to training
  • Annual audit of EYFS provision and environments
  • Coaching of EYFS Leaders
  • EYFS curriculum developments
  • Supporting QA in schools
  • Attending and accessing DfE and LA briefings to provide guidance to schools

Primary Attendance Lead

Celia Smith

Celia supports schools to achieve excellent attendance through disseminating information from the attendance strategy, working directly with schools on their systems and processes, and supporting individual children whose attendance is stuck, dropping or below target.

  • Disseminating information and guidance from the Trust attendance strategy
  • Working directly with schools on attendance systems and processes
  • Supporting individual children whose attendance is stuck, dropping or below target
  • Providing CPD, coaching and support to attendance leads across Nova primaries
